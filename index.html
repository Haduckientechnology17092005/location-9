HOI AN TRAVEL TOUR GUIDE

Hoi An Night Market & Boat Trip & Lantern Releasing : (price : 1.000.000VND)

You will discover the bustling shopping space in Hoi An night market and immerse yourself in extraordinary local culture on this trip by night boat. Travel through the city, and feel the magical sensation of releasing your own lantern into the river. Let's enjoy my trip.

Details:

Pickup

Pick up at the Meeting Point (Godiva - Dinks & Cakes at 11 Hoang Dieu Street, Hoi An ) and my lovely guide takes you to the Hoi An Old Town.
Sightseeing the Japanese Covered Bridge; the Assembly Hall ; Hoi An Traditional Art Performance House; Tan Ky Old House.

Action

Visit Hoi An night market, explore Hoi An at its most magical during an evening boat ride along Hoai River and release the lantern into the river.
Visit the Hoi An Moonlight Bridge is the walking bridge has a unique design that is inspired from the monthly full moon festival in Hoi An. You have chance to see and photograph a world of colorful lights floating lanterns on the river.
